How to use the website

This is a website centered around making your life easier inside and outside the kitchen with a search tool capable of finding a recipe catering to your needs, a submission page allowing for an infinitely expanding database, and a meal plan feature to take the daily uncertaincy out of your life.

By using the search tool you can input anything from a name, a price, or a list of ingredients to find a list of recipes catered to your needs.

Can't find a recipe you want? By using our recipe submission page our database will never cease to expand until there is a recipe for every occasion.

By using our meal plan feature you can remove the daily struggle of what you're going to eat while also allowing for once grocery trip with no extra wasted materials.
