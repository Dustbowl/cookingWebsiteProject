Lemon Parsley Pasta
8 oz. thin spaghetti 1 fresh lemon 2 cloves garlic, minced 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley 1/8 tsp freshly cracked black pepper 1 Tbsp olive oil 2 Tbsp butter 2 Tbsp grated Parmesan 1/4 tsp salt
3
Boil large pot of water, add the pasta until tender (about 7-8 minutes). Drain the pasta in a colander. While the pasta is cooking, zest the lemon 1 Tbsp. Chop parsley and combine it with the Parmesan, lemon zest, and pepper in a bowl. While the pasta is draining, add the olive oil and minced garlic to the pot used to cook the pasta. Sauté the garlic in the oil over medium heat for 1-2 minutes, or just until it becomes fragrant. Remove pot from the heat and add the drained pasta and butter to the pot with the garlic. Toss the pasta until the residual heat has melted the butter and the pasta is coated in garlic and butter. Add the parsley Parmesan mixture to the pot and toss to coat again. Squeeze about 1 Tbsp of the lemon juice over the pasta and season with salt. 

Garlic Parmesan Kale Pasta
1 bunch 1/2 lb kale 1/2 lb angel hair pasta 2 Tbsp olive oil 2 Tbsp butter 2 cloves garlic, minced 1/4 cup grated Parmesan Pinch salt and pepper 
5
Pull kale leaves from stems and tear them into small 1-2 inch pieces. Rinse the kale well in a colander under cool, running water and allow it to drain. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Break the pasta in half, add it to the boiling water, and cook about 7 minutes. Drain the pasta in a colander. While the pasta is cooking, add the olive oil, butter, and minced garlic to another large pot or skillet. Cook over medium heat for 1-2 minutes, or until the garlic is soft and fragrant. Add the kale and continue to sauté until the kale has wilted and has turned a deep green color or about 5-7 minutes. Turn the heat off. Add the drained pasta to the pot with the sautéed kale. Toss the pasta and kale together. Allow them to cool. You want the pasta warm, but not hot enough to melt the Parmesan. Season the pasta and kale with salt and pepper. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and toss to coat.

Garlic Parmesan Roasted Cauliflower
1 head califlower, 1/4 cup grated parmesan, 1/2 tsp garlic powder, 1/4 tsp salt, 1/4 tsp pepper, 2 tbsp olive oil, 1 tbsp chopped parsley (optional)
3
Preheat oven to 400F Rinse cauliflower well, cut into bite size pieces. Combine parmesan, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in small bowl. Place cauliflower pieces in a bowl and drizzle olive oil on top then the parmesan mixture, then toss until covered. Spread cauliflower over baking sheet, transfer into preheated oven. Roast cauliflower for 30 minutes stirring halfway through.

Chocolate Banana Muffins
1 cup of mashed bananas, 1 cup of yogurt, 2 eggs, 1/2 cup sugar, 4 tbsp of butter, 1 tsp of vanilla extract, 1.5 cups all purpose flour, 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder, 1 tsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp baking soda, 1/2 tsp salt, 1/2 cup chocolate chips
4 
Preheat oven to 350. Line muffin tin with paper liners. Mash bananas well and add to mixing bowl with yogurt eggs sugar butter and vanilla. Whisk together well. In a seperate bowl stir flower, cocoa powder baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Combine the two bowls and stir until combined. Fold chocolate chips into batter. Divide batter between the cups of the muffin pan. Transfer to oven and bake for 35 minutes. 

Garlic Butter Shrimp
1/2 lbs shrimp, 2 cloves garlic, 1 lemon, 2 tbsp butter, 1 tbsp olive oil, 1/8 tsp salt, 1/8 tsp pepper, 1 tsp of parsley (optional)
4 
Allow shrimp to thaw, remove the shell and tail and pat dry. Squeeze two tablespoons of juice from lemon and mince garlic. Add Butter and olive oil into a skillet over medium heat until butter begins to foam. Add shrimp to skillet and cook until each side is pink ~1-3 minutes each side. Add garlic and 1 Tbsp of lemon juice to skillet, continue to cook shrimp in garlic butter for one more minute or until the raw edge is cooked out of the garlic. Season shrimp with salt pepper and parsley.

Chedder Drop Biscuits
2.25 cups all purpose flower, 1 tsp salt, 1 tbsp baking powder, 1/4 tsp garlic powder, 1 tsp sugar, 4 oz chedder cheese, 3 green onions, 1 pint heavy whipping cream
3
Preheat oven to 400, Line baking sheet with parchment paper. In a bowl stir flour salt baking powder garlic powder and sugar until combined. Add shredded cheddar and sliced green onions to the flour mixture and stir to combine again. Add the heavy cream and stir until batter forms. Spoon batter onto parchment sheet. 12 biscuits. Bake for 18-20 minutes.
